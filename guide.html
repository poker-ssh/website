How To Play - Poker

This game my be slightly different to normal poker

Terminology

Check: Pass the action to the next person Bet __: The first bet in a betting phase Raise __: Betting more money than the initial bet or raise Fold: Forfeit your hand Call: To bet the same amount as the highest bet Pot: All the money that was bet in all the betting phases All in: Betting all the money you have

Hand Rankings

From best to worst: Royal Flush: 10, J, Q, K, A, all of the same suit as a straight Example: [10♦] [J♦] [Q♦] [K♦] [A♦] Straight Flush: Five cards in sequence, all of the same suit Example: [5♠] [6♠] [7♠] [8♠] [9♠] Four of a Kind: Four cards of the same rank Example: [7♠] [7♦] [7♥] [7♣] Full House: Three of a kind + pair Example: [5♦] [5♠] [9♣] [9♠] [9♥] Flush: Five cards of the same suit Example: [4♠] [Q♠] [A♠] [7♠] [9♠] Straight: Five cards in sequence Example: [8♥] [9♣] [10♦] [J♠] [Q♠] Three of a Kind: Three cards of the same rank Example: [K♠] [K♥] [K♣] Two Pair: Two different pairs Example: [3♦] [3♣] [6♥] [6♣] Pair: Two cards of the same rank Example: [J♥] [J♠] High Card: Highest card wins

Stages

Poker has 5 stages: Pre-flop, Flop, Turn, River and Showdown

Pre-flop

This is the start of the game where everyone is given 2 cards (a hand). With these 2 cards, in the future, you would need to make the best hand out of a maximum of 5 cards using the community cards (flop, turn, river). After being dealt the hand, a betting phase occurs where everyone needs to put in money. The first person has to bet a minimum of $1, and everyone else needs to either call, raise or fold. At the end of the betting phase, everyone should have bet the same amount except for anyone who has folded.

Flop

Following the Pre-Flop comes the Flop, where the dealer will reveal 3 of the 5 community cards. Then another betting phase occurs, but instead of there being a minimum bet of $1, you are given an option to check, bet or fold.

Turn

Next come the Turn where the dealer will show the 4th card of the 5 community cards. Again, another betting phase occurs with the same rules as the betting phase in the flop.

River

Afterwards comes the River, where the dealer will show the 5th and last community card that you can make your best hand with. The last betting phase of the round occurs.

Showdown

After all the stages, the last stage is the showdown. In the showdown, everyone shows their cards, and whoever has the best hand wins the entire pot. If 2 or more people have the same hand (eg, 2 people have both King and Queen, two pair (no matter the suit), the pot will be split between the people with the same hand.
